HailDental

A dental health app UI. To bridge communication gaps between patients and dentists.

The Goal

HailDental is a mobile application that allows patients to communicate their symptoms clearly to dentists, as well as keep track of their own oral health. The personalized feed and tips page also helps shape user behavior to practice healthy dental practices.

Our goal is to close the communication gap between patients and dentists.

Hunt Statement

We aim to create a mobile health application that promotes healthy dental routines to college students and allows for easy communication regarding pain between patients and dentists.

RESEARCH

Methods

Google Form Survey for students


  1. Google Form Survey for students
  2. Interviews with students that we approached on campus (around 15 min each, 10F, 2M)
  3. Interviews with 3 U of M clinicians that we initially emailed

  • -Advertise different dental costs
  • -Making insurance information easily accessible
  • -Tracking diet
